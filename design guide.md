# Mensah Brand Guidelines

## Brand Essence
**Tagline:** *"Crafted for the Exceptional"*

Mensah is a luxury tailored menswear brand rooted in precision, heritage, and quiet confidence. Every design decision should whisper exclusivity — never shout it.

---Here's the full brand system for Mensah. A few key decisions behind the choices:

**Color logic** — the palette is built around natural materials: Obsidian (deep black), Ivory and Linen (natural cloth), and a single Gold accent. This mirrors how Gucci and Hermès use restraint — one warm accent against neutrals does more than a full palette.

**Typography** — Cormorant Garamond at Light weight for all display and headings gives you that tall, refined editorial feel. Tracked uppercase sans-serif handles all UI labels and navigation. Never mix more than these two.

**Logo** — no icon, no emblem. The wordmark alone, widely spaced, is the mark. Luxury brands like Celine and The Row prove that a clean letterform outperforms a logo symbol.

**Buttons** — sharp corners only, no border-radius. Rounded buttons read as friendly/approachable; squared edges read as precise and tailored — right for Mensah.

**Gold** — used strictly as an accent for CTAs and key highlights. Overusing it turns luxury into gaudy.
