Design Rules

Do

Use generous whitespace — luxury breathes
Keep copy minimal and deliberate
Favour tracked uppercase for labels
Use Obsidian backgrounds for impact
Gold accents for CTAs and highlights only

Don't

No bright or saturated colors
No rounded buttons — sharp edges only
No heavy fonts above weight 400
No busy backgrounds or textures
Never stack more than 2 typefaces

Motion & Interaction

Transitions: 400ms ease — slow and intentional, never snappy
Hover states: subtle opacity shift (1.0 → 0.65), no color flashes
Page entrances: fade-in with a slight upward drift (translateY 8px → 0)
Buttons: no fill on hover — border brightens slightly to #C8BCA8
Skeleton loaders: pulse on Charcoal (#1C1C1A), never a spinner
